Mechanical Engineering Core Jobs

Mechanical Design Engineer

Designs mechanical systems and products such as machines, tools, engines, and HVAC systems.

Manufacturing Engineer

Focuses on the design, operation, and optimization of manufacturing processes and systems.

Thermal Engineer

Specializes in heat transfer, thermodynamics, and energy systems.

Structural Engineer

Designs and analyzes structural components of buildings, bridges, and mechanical systems.

CAD Engineer

Utilizes CAD software to create blueprints, schematics, and prototypes for mechanical components.

Robotics Engineer

Designs and develops robotic systems for manufacturing, healthcare, and consumer products.

Automotive Engineer

Works in the automotive industry designing and developing vehicles and vehicle components.

Project Engineer

Manages and coordinates engineering projects from start to finish.

Maintenance Engineer

Ensures that mechanical equipment and machinery are functioning properly.

Material Engineer

Works with different materials (metals, polymers, ceramics) to select the appropriate material for engineering projects.
